# Trust, Signaling, and Public Opportunity Quotes

## Mark Granovetter

> "Stress is laid on the cohesive power of weak ties."

Why it matters here:
This is the tightest line for the argument that opportunity depends on circulation, not only on skill possession.

Source:

- [The Strength of Weak Ties](https://www.journals.uchicago.edu/doi/10.1086/225469)

## NIST AI RMF

> "voluntary, rights-preserving, non-sector specific, and use-case agnostic"

Why it matters here:
This short phrase gives the project a compact governance reference when discussing responsible AI framing.

Source:

- [Artificial Intelligence Risk Management Framework (AI RMF 1.0)](https://www.nist.gov/publications/artificial-intelligence-risk-management-framework-ai-rmf-10)

## Usage notes

1. Granovetter works best in public deployment, networking, and portfolio circulation sections.
2. The NIST line works best in governance sections and responsible-AI pages, not in student inspiration copy.
