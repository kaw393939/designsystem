# Motivation and Development Quotes

## Abraham Maslow

> "What a man can be, he must be."

Why it matters here:
This is the cleanest short line for the project's growth and potential frame.

Source:

- [A Theory of Human Motivation](https://psychclassics.yorku.ca/Maslow/motivation.htm)

## Richard Ryan and Edward Deci

> "SDT represents a broad framework for understanding why we do what we do."

Why it matters here:
Use this when the project needs a concise motivation frame that is social, developmental, and not limited to compliance.

Source:

- [Self-Determination Theory overview](https://selfdeterminationtheory.org/the-theory/)
- [Ryan and Deci 2000 PDF](https://www.selfdeterminationtheory.org/SDT/documents/2000_RyanDeci_SDT.pdf)

## Usage notes

1. Maslow works best in project framing, mission language, and student aspiration sections.
2. Ryan and Deci work best in curriculum doctrine, teaching rationale, and coaching arguments.
