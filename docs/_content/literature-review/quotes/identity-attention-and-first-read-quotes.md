# Identity, Attention, and First-Read Quotes

## Lindgaard et al

> "You have 50 milliseconds to make a good first impression!"

Why it matters here:
This is the shortest high-impact line for the argument that page perception is immediate and structured by first-read cues.

Source:

- [Attention web-paper PDF](https://www.makinggood.ac.nz/media/1265/lindegaardetal_2006_attention.pdf)

## Usage notes

1. Use this in page-design lessons, homepage critiques, and proof for fast-judgment claims.
2. Keep the quote paired with the broader attention literature so it is not treated as a standalone law.
