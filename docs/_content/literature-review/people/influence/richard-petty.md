# Richard Petty

## Why he matters here

Petty is one of the core sources for understanding why some messages are processed deeply while others are handled through quicker cues.

He matters whenever the project has to explain why attention, motivation, and argument strength affect conversion.

## What this project should take

1. Message processing depth varies.
2. Strong arguments matter when scrutiny is high.
3. Peripheral cues matter when time or motivation is low.

## What to handle carefully

1. Do not reduce every page decision to one persuasion model.
2. Keep elaboration tied to audience context and stakes.
