# Daniel Kahneman

## Why he matters here

Kahneman gives the corpus a wider judgment-and-uncertainty frame behind many persuasion claims.

He matters because first-read evaluation is shaped by shortcuts, framing, and bounded attention rather than only deliberate reasoning.

## What this project should take

1. People use heuristics under uncertainty.
2. Framing shapes judgment.
3. Decision quality is not explained by rational deliberation alone.

## What to handle carefully

1. Do not use biases language as a total explanation for all behavior.
2. Do not turn cognitive limitations into a license for manipulation.
