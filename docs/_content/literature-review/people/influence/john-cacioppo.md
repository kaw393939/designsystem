# John Cacioppo

## Why he matters here

Cacioppo matters because the project needs a serious account of persuasion that includes cognition, effort, and variable scrutiny.

He helps keep the trust layer grounded in how people actually process messages.

## What this project should take

1. Attention and motivation change how messages are evaluated.
2. Some decisions depend on cues, while others depend on argument quality.
3. Persuasion structure must fit audience state.

## What to handle carefully

1. Do not oversimplify the model into central equals good and peripheral equals bad.
2. Keep the theory connected to page context and time pressure.
