# Amos Tversky

## Why he matters here

Tversky helps explain the judgment shortcuts, framing effects, and uncertainty conditions that shape response to messages and choices.

He matters for the same reason Kahneman matters, but with particular value for the structure of decision framing.

## What this project should take

1. Judgment under uncertainty is structured by heuristics.
2. Framing changes response.
3. Decision environments shape outcomes.

## What to handle carefully

1. Do not detach heuristics work from context and task demands.
2. Keep the theory descriptive, not morally opportunistic.
