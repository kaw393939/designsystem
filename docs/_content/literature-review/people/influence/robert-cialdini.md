# Robert Cialdini

## Why he matters here

Cialdini gives the corpus the most usable practical language for influence under uncertainty.

He matters when the project needs to explain why page structure, proof, and social cues affect action.

## What this project should take

1. Influence levers are practical when tied to reality.
2. Social proof, authority, commitment, reciprocity, liking, scarcity, and unity shape response.
3. Influence works best when embedded in structure.

## What to handle carefully

1. Do not use influence language to rationalize manipulation.
2. Keep every influence tactic tied to real proof.
