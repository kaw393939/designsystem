# Edward Deci

## Why he matters here

Deci is essential to the project's motivation layer because Self-Determination Theory is not only a framework for desire, but also a framework for educational conditions.

He matters whenever the project argues that students need feedback, structure, and agency together.

## What this project should take

1. Motivation quality matters as much as motivation quantity.
2. External controls can suppress better forms of engagement.
3. Competence grows through evidence, feedback, and supported challenge.

## What to handle carefully

1. Do not treat intrinsic motivation as the only legitimate form of motivation.
2. Do not turn competence into vague encouragement language.
3. Keep Deci linked to actual teaching design, not only theory summaries.
