# David Kenrick

## Why he matters here

Kenrick helps modernize the motivation layer by showing that the project does not need a rigid staircase model to keep need language useful.

He matters when the corpus needs a more flexible, life-stage-aware account of motive systems.

## What this project should take

1. Motives are dynamic and context sensitive.
2. Multiple systems can be active together.
3. Human behavior is not best explained by a single fixed sequence.

## What to handle carefully

1. Do not let evolutionary framing erase culture or institutions.
2. Do not use the update to discard the practical value of need categories.
