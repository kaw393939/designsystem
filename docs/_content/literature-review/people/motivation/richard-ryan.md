# Richard Ryan

## Why he matters here

Ryan is one of the core builders of Self-Determination Theory, which gives the project a stronger educational and developmental foundation than Maslow alone.

He matters whenever the corpus argues that good learning environments support agency rather than merely extract compliance.

## What this project should take

1. Autonomy, competence, and relatedness are central motivational supports.
2. Social context can support or thwart development.
3. High-quality motivation is different from external compliance.

## What to handle carefully

1. Do not use autonomy to mean absence of structure.
2. Do not reduce competence to confidence language without evidence.
3. Keep the theory social and environmental, not purely internal.

## Verified short quote

> "SDT represents a broad framework for understanding why we do what we do."
