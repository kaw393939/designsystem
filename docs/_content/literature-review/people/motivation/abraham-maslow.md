# Abraham Maslow

## Why he matters here

Maslow gives the project its first vocabulary for need, deprivation, growth, and self-actualization.

He matters most at the entry point of the system, where students have to ask what human need their work is answering.

## What this project should take

1. Need matters before feature language.
2. Motivation is layered rather than singular.
3. Growth motives matter, not only survival motives.
4. More than one motive can be active at the same time.

## What to handle carefully

1. Do not teach the pyramid as a rigid universal ladder.
2. Do not use self-actualization as empty prestige language.
3. Keep Maslow paired with later motivation research so the project stays defensible.

## Verified short quote

> "What a man can be, he must be."
