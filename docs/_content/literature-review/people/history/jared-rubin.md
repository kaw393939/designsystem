# Jared Rubin

## Why he matters here

Rubin strengthens the argument that print was tied to religious and institutional change, not only knowledge growth.

He matters when the project wants a more political and institutional reading of media-system shifts.

## What this project should take

1. Media systems can alter religious and institutional power.
2. Knowledge revolutions are also legitimacy revolutions.
3. Institutional change should be explained through real historical mechanisms.

## What to handle carefully

1. Do not use one line of Reformation history as a universal template.
2. Keep Rubin inside a broader historical stack with Eisenstein and Anderson.
