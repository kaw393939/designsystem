# Jeremiah Dittmar

## Why he matters here

Dittmar provides empirical support for the claim that print adoption had long-run economic effects.

He matters because the project needs more than metaphor when it argues that media shifts reshape economic and institutional outcomes.

## What this project should take

1. Media adoption can have measurable long-run effects.
2. Institutional change can be studied empirically, not only narratively.
3. Historical comparisons are stronger when grounded in outcome data.

## What to handle carefully

1. Do not overstate one historical dataset as total proof.
2. Keep empirical findings paired with broader cultural interpretation.
