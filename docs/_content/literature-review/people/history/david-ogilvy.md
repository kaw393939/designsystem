# David Ogilvy

## Why he matters here

Ogilvy matters because the project needs communication that works in public, not only theory that sounds intelligent in private.

He strengthens the corpus on proof, clarity, headline discipline, and evidence-led messaging.

## What this project should take

1. Communication should do a job.
2. Clear claims and proof matter more than decorative cleverness.
3. Public-facing artifacts should earn attention and action.

## What to handle carefully

1. Do not inherit outdated advertising assumptions uncritically.
2. Keep Ogilvy tied to ethical influence and contemporary audience literacy.
