# Josef Muller-Brockmann

## Why he matters here

Muller-Brockmann matters because the project treats clarity, hierarchy, and grid logic as part of public reasoning rather than cosmetic design taste.

He helps the corpus defend visual order as cognitive infrastructure.

1. Grids are clarity infrastructure.
2. Structure improves legibility.
3. Visual order can support public reasoning.

## What to handle carefully

1. Do not reduce design quality to sterile grid worship.
2. Keep formal order tied to human attention and proof.
