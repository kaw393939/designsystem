# Mark Granovetter

## Why he matters here

Granovetter explains why weak ties matter for diffusion, mobility, and job information.

He matters because the project insists that good work must circulate publicly to produce opportunity.

## What this project should take

1. Strong ties often circulate redundant information.
2. Weak ties bridge groups and carry novel opportunities.
3. Opportunity depends on circulation, not only skill possession.

## What to handle carefully

1. Do not romanticize networking without substantive work.
2. Keep weak-tie strategy tied to real public proof.

## Verified short quote

> "Stress is laid on the cohesive power of weak ties."
