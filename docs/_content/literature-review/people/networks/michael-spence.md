# Michael Spence

## Why he matters here

Spence explains why signals matter when direct information is incomplete.

He matters because the portfolio in this project functions partly as a market-facing signal of capability.

## What this project should take

1. Observable signals matter when deeper quality is hidden.
2. Educational and portfolio artifacts can function as signals.
3. Signal quality changes inference quality.

## What to handle carefully

1. Do not reduce human value to credential theater.
2. Do not let signaling substitute for real capability.
