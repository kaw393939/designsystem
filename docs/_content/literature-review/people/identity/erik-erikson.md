# Erik Erikson

## Why he matters here

Erikson gives the project a developmental account of identity rather than a purely stylistic one.

He matters when the corpus needs to explain why youth and early adulthood are especially intense periods of identity construction.

## What this project should take

1. Identity formation is a developmental task.
2. Social context matters to identity stability.
3. Early adult formation has real pressure and consequence.

## What to handle carefully

1. Do not flatten Erikson into self-help slogans.
2. Do not strip away the social pressures built into his theory.
