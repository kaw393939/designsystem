# Margaret Mark

## Why she matters here

Mark is the public-communication bridge between archetype language and brand strategy.

She matters when the project moves from identity theory into homepage clarity, symbolic framing, and consistent public signal.

## What this project should take

1. Archetypes are useful when they sharpen meaning.
2. A stable signal is easier to read than a noisy one.
3. Public positioning should be coherent before it becomes complex.

## What to handle carefully

1. Do not treat brand archetypes as scientific destiny.
2. Keep the method subordinate to evidence, audience, and proof.
