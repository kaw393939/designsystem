# Carl Jung

## Why he matters here

Jung is the symbolic source for archetype language in the corpus.

He matters because the project uses archetypes as recurring public story forms, not as a personality-test product.

## What this project should take

1. Archetypes can explain recurring symbolic patterns.
2. Public identity is often interpreted through familiar narrative forms.
3. Symbolic coherence can increase legibility.

## What to handle carefully

1. Do not present Jung as a modern empirical classification system.
2. Do not use archetypes as destiny claims about a person.
3. Keep the project focused on communication coherence, not depth psychology cosplay.
