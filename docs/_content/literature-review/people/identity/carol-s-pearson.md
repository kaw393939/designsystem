# Carol S. Pearson

## Why she matters here

Pearson helps translate archetype language into public communication and brand coherence.

She matters because the project needs archetypes to function as narrative constraints for pages and portfolios, not as abstract theory alone.

## What this project should take

1. Archetypes can clarify public meaning.
2. The goal is coherence, not total personal description.
3. Symbolic framing can help a page read faster.

## What to handle carefully

1. Do not overclaim empirical validation for every branding use case.
2. Do not let archetype choice replace audience analysis or proof.
