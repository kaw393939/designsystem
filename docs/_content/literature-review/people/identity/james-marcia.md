# James Marcia

## Why he matters here

Marcia makes Erikson more operational for teaching and analysis by translating identity formation into statuses tied to exploration and commitment.

He matters when the project needs a clearer language for stable, unstable, or underdeveloped public identity.

## What this project should take

1. Commitment matters.
2. Exploration matters.
3. Diffusion is a useful concept for unstable public identity.

## What to handle carefully

1. Do not use identity status as a fixed label for a person.
2. Keep the model developmental and revisable.
