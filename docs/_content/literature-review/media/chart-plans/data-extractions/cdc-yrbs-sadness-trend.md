# CDC YRBS: Persistent Sadness and Hopelessness Trend

## Source

CDC Youth Risk Behavior Survey Data Summary and Trends Report, 2013-2023.

Source URL: <https://www.cdc.gov/mmwr/volumes/73/su/su7304a9.htm>

Retrieval date: 2026-04-05

## Measure

Percent of U.S. high school students who felt so sad or hopeless almost every day for at least two weeks in a row that they stopped doing some usual activities, during the past 12 months.

## Data

| Year | Percent |
| --- | --- |
| 2011 | 28 |
| 2013 | 30 |
| 2015 | 30 |
| 2017 | 31 |
| 2019 | 37 |
| 2021 | 42 |
| 2023 | 40 |

## Chart recommendation

Line chart showing the upward trend from 28% (2011) to 42% (2021) with a slight decrease to 40% (2023).
