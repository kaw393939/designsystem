# McKinsey: Generative AI Value by Business Function

## Source

McKinsey Global Institute, The Economic Potential of Generative AI: The Next Productivity Frontier (June 2023).

Source URL: <https://www.mckinsey.com/capabilities/mckinsey-digital/our-insights/the-economic-potential-of-generative-ai-the-next-productivity-frontier>

Retrieval date: 2026-04-05

## Headline

Total generative AI potential across 63 use cases: $2.6-$4.4 trillion per year globally.

About 75% of that value comes from four functions.

## Data: Value by function (billions per year)

| Business function | Low estimate | High estimate |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Marketing and sales | 760 | 1200 |
| Software engineering | 580 | 1200 |
| Supply chain and operations | 280 | 530 |
| Customer operations | 340 | 470 |
| Product R&D | 230 | 420 |
| Risk and legal | 180 | 260 |
| Corporate IT | 60 | 90 |
| Strategy and finance | 40 | 50 |

Note: ranges exclude implementation costs. Total could roughly double if gen AI is broadly embedded into existing software.

## Chart recommendation

Horizontal range-bar chart sorted by midpoint value, showing the spread between low and high estimates.

The top four functions should be visually grouped or highlighted to reinforce the 75% concentration claim.
