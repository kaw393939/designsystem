# ACT: College Readiness Benchmark Attainment

## Source

ACT Condition of College and Career Readiness reports, Classes of 2023 and 2024.

Source URL: <https://www.act.org/content/act/en/research/condition-of-college-and-career-readiness.html>

Retrieval date: 2026-04-05

## Measure

Percent of ACT-tested graduates meeting the college readiness benchmark in each subject.

Benchmarks: English 18, Reading 22, Math 22, Science 23.

## Data

| Subject | Class of 2023 | Class of 2024 |
| --- | --- | --- |
| English | 51 | 51 |
| Reading | 40 | 40 |
| Math | 30 | 29 |
| Science | 31 | 30 |

## Chart recommendation

Grouped bar chart with one group per subject, two bars per group (2023 vs 2024).

Highlight the gap between English (51%) and Math/Science (29-30%) to emphasize the readiness problem.
