# NAEP: Reading Score Trend

## Source

National Center for Education Statistics, NAEP Reading Assessments.

Source URL: <https://nces.ed.gov/nationsreportcard/reading/>

Retrieval date: 2026-04-05

## Measure A: Main NAEP reading average scale scores (national public schools)

| Grade | 2022 | 2024 | Change |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Grade 4 | 216 | 214 | -2 |
| Grade 8 | 259 | 257 | -2 |

Note: Grade 4 average in 2024 (214) is not significantly different from the first Main NAEP reading assessment in 1992.

## Measure B: Long-Term Trend (LTT) NAEP reading (age-based)

| Age group | 1971 | 2020 | 2022/2023 | Change from 2020 |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Age 9 | 208 | 220 | 215 (2022) | -5 |
| Age 13 | 255 | 260 | 256 (2023) | -4 |

Note: Age 13 reading in 2023 has returned to approximately the 1971 level (255 vs 256).

## Chart recommendation

Dual-panel line chart. Left panel: Main NAEP Grade 4 and Grade 8 from 1992 to 2024. Right panel: LTT Age 9 and Age 13 from 1971 to 2023.

Highlight the post-pandemic drop and the return to 1970s/1990s baselines.
