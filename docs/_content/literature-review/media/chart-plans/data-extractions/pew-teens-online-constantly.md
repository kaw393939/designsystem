# Pew: Teens Online Almost Constantly

## Source

Pew Research Center, Teens, Social Media and Technology reports (2022, 2023, 2024).

Source URL: <https://www.pewresearch.org/internet/2024/12/12/teens-social-media-and-technology-2024/>

Retrieval date: 2026-04-05

## Measure A: Online almost constantly by age group

| Year | All teens | Ages 13-14 | Ages 15-17 |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 2022 | 46 | 36 | 52 |
| 2023 | 46 | 40 | 50 |
| 2024 | 46 | 38 | 50 |

Survey fielded: 2022 (Apr-May), 2023 (Sep-Oct), 2024 (Sep-Oct).

## Measure B: Use each platform almost constantly (all teens)

| Platform | 2022 | 2023 | 2024 |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| YouTube | 19 | 16 | 15 |
| TikTok | 16 | 17 | 16 |
| Snapchat | 15 | 14 | 13 |
| Instagram | 10 | 8 | 12 |
| Facebook | 2 | 3 | 3 |

Note: percentages are of all teens, not just platform users.

## Chart recommendation

Measure A: grouped horizontal bars by age band, one group per year.

Measure B: small-multiples bar chart, one bar per platform per year, showing stability and slight shifts.
