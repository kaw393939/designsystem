# Branding and System Prompts

## Purpose

These prompts explore brand direction for the future research site or family of sites.

## Prompt 1: Research atlas board

Goal:
Find a visual language for a serious, modern research archive that is not generic SaaS minimalism.

Prompt:
"Brand board for an ambitious research and education studio, combining editorial typography specimens, archival paper textures, red pencil marks, system diagrams, understated interface fragments, disciplined grid, cream, ink black, oxidized red, muted teal, intelligent and human, premium magazine-meets-lab aesthetic, no text"

Recommended generation:
Landscape, high quality.

## Prompt 2: Identity portfolio signal poster

Goal:
Create a signature art direction reference for the identity portfolio concept.

Prompt:
"Poster-style brand visual for an identity portfolio system, blending portrait fragments, project proof artifacts, directional lines, geometric panels, and tactile print textures, bold editorial composition, contemporary but not corporate, confident use of negative space, no text"

Recommended generation:
Portrait, high quality.

## Prompt 3: Second renaissance cover image

Goal:
Develop a hero image direction for long-form essays and manifesto pages.

Prompt:
"Book-cover-style editorial composition for a second renaissance theme, fusion of Renaissance engraving energy and contemporary computational diagrams, gold, carbon black, parchment, and dark oxide palette, atmospheric but precise, intellectually ambitious, no text"

Recommended generation:
Portrait, high quality.
