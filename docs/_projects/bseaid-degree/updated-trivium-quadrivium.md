# Updated Trivium and Quadrivium

## Status

- Status: working concept note
- Purpose: provide a human-formation model that can sit underneath the BSEAID degree and the first student-facing site

## Working claim

The historical comparison becomes much stronger if the degree can say:

We are not only teaching tools.
We are updating the old formation disciplines for an AI-shaped world.

## Updated trivium

The modern trivium should govern the student's relationship to language, models, and meaning.

### 1. Read

Students must learn to read:

1. sources
2. prompts and outputs
3. specs and constraints
4. institutions and situations

### 2. Write

Students must learn to write:

1. prompts
2. documentation
3. product narratives
4. stakeholder explanations
5. public proofs of work

### 3. Reason

Students must learn to reason:

1. under uncertainty
2. across evidence and ambiguity
3. about risks and tradeoffs
4. about what a system should and should not do

## Updated quadrivium

The modern quadrivium should govern the student's relationship to measurement, computation, systems, and public form.

### 1. Quantify

Statistics, measurement, evaluation, experimentation, and model judgment.

### 2. Compute

Programming, automation, software construction, and technical fluency.

### 3. Orchestrate

Workflows, agents, tools, retrieval, infrastructure, and multi-system coordination.

### 4. Represent

Design, interface, media, visualization, public explanation, and signal shaping.

## Why this matters

This model gives the degree a cleaner answer to a hard question:

What kind of person are we trying to form?

Answer:

A person who can read, write, reason, quantify, compute, orchestrate, and represent responsibly in public.

## How this connects to the first site

The first site is the earliest practical laboratory for this model.

Students practice:

1. reading themselves and their audience
2. writing signal and proof
3. reasoning about identity and fit
4. representing themselves in public form

## How this connects to the degree

The full BSEAID program should repeatedly cycle students through all seven capacities rather than isolating them in separate silos.

## Open design question

Decide whether this framework stays as internal program doctrine only or becomes part of the student-facing narrative for the degree.
