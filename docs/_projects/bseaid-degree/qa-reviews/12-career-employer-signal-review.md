# Career and Employer-Signal Review

## Status

- Lens: career, employer-signal, or professional-practice reviewer
- Review target: credibility of outputs to external audiences

## Findings

1. The artifact ladder is promising, but the IA does not yet define a compact external-credibility rubric for exemplars and proof blocks.
2. The publish and deployment layer is strong conceptually, but room-specific evidence expectations could be more concrete.
3. The current outputs are legible internally, but the IA should make clearer what an outside reviewer should be able to infer from each artifact.

## Risks

1. Pages may feel polished without proving the right things to employers, clients, or collaborators.
2. Students may deploy weak artifacts into rooms that demand stronger proof.
3. The program may rely on internal logic rather than external readability.

## Required changes

1. Add a public-proof rubric defining what makes an artifact externally credible.
2. Add room-matching guidance for what kind of evidence belongs in builder, workshop, networking, governance, and career rooms.
3. Add an outside-reviewer test that asks what a stranger can infer in under a minute.
